Open bookmark folder - options

Changing the identifier ("ob")

To change the identifier:

  1. Right click the omnibox (address bar) and select "Edit Search Engines"
  2. Scroll to the bottom
  3. Under the section for keywords for installed extensions, find the "Open bookmark folder" extension and click the keyword value to the right of it's name. There is no edit button, it will turn into an editable field.
